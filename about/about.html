About 9ja News Pluse

9ja News pluse is a fast-growing digital media platform focused on delivering accurate, timely, and engaging news stories from Nigeria and around the world.

We cover a wide range of topics including politics, sports, entertainment, business, and trending stories that matter to everyday Nigerians.

Our mission is simple — to keep you informed with reliable updates while presenting content in a clean, fast, and mobile-friendly experience.

Our Vision

To become one of Nigeria’s most trusted online news platforms by delivering high-quality journalism and real-time updates.

Editorial Standards

We aim to provide accurate and fair reporting. While we strive for correctness, readers are encouraged to verify information from official sources when necessary.
